Library

Our library is open to all people regardless of age, religion and gender,

it provides a conducive and safe environment for many children & youth where they come to conduct their studies, reading and research through access to general and school textbooks & publications We also provide computers for research and access to e-book material from the internet The library operates from Monday to Saturday from 9:00 AM-5:00 PM.
